Mines in Santa Cruz County
I reported on the dramatic impact mining has had on the Patagonia mountains in Southern Arizona. Using records from USGS, I built this animated map showing how mining boomed and then collapsed in the region. I produced the animation using MapBox, Carto and After Effects. The project was part of my thesis at UC Berkeley's Graduate School of Journalism.
