How We Identified the Frat Brothers Holding Guns at an Emmett Till Memorial
I produced this explantory video for ProPublica's Evidence series. The source material largely came from a social media investigation. I used GeoLayers and geospatial data to build the maps. I wrote the script, tracked the voice over and produced all of the graphics.

This video was published in a multimedia package that examined deadly high speed car chases involving Border Patrol. I shot the footage on a Mavic Pro and designed the locator map and text in After Effects.

Unmasking California's New White Supremacists
I edited and produced this video that identified several members of a white supremacist group for the first time. The project involved reviewing hours of user-generated footage of violent protests across California and in Charlottesville, VA. The video was viewed more than 12 million times on Facebook. Four of the men identified in the video have since been arrested on rioting charges.

The video was published with this investigation by ProPublica.

Deadnamed
I reported and wrote this story with colleague Ken Schwencke. We looked into every documented murder of a transgender person in the United States over a three-year period and found that in 87 percent of cases the victim was deadnamed or misgendered by law enforcement. Our story focused on Jacksonville, where three murders of black transgender women in the first six months of 2018 remain unsolved.

How ID Laws Can Put Trans People In Danger
I co-produced this explainer video with Vox on how onerous ID laws make it difficult, and sometimes impossible, for transgender people to change their name or gender marker on a state ID.

Inside the Secret Chat Logs of American Nazis
I created this video after ProPublica obtained a trove of secret chats between members of the neo-Nazi group Atomwaffen Division. We revealed that the accused killer of Blaze Bernstein, a gay Jewish college student, was a member of the group and identified other group leaders.

How A Police Beating in Elkhart, IN Unfolded
I produced this video with ProPublica and the South Bend Tribune using footage obtained through record requests. I edited the final product within six hours of receiving the raw video file.

Following the release of this video and other stories about police misconduct in Elkhart, the police chief resigned.

Mines in Santa Cruz County
I reported on the dramatic impact mining has had on the Patagonia mountains in Southern Arizona. Using records from USGS, I built this animated map showing how mining boomed and then collapsed in the region. I produced the animation using MapBox, Carto and After Effects. The project was part of my thesis project at UC Berkeley's Graduate School of Journalism.

A Town Divided
I reported and produced this short documentary about how new mining projects have bitterly divided the tiny town of Patagonia, AZ. The film was part of my thesis project at UC Berkeley's Graduate School of Journalism.
